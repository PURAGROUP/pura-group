What We Do

From Idea to Running Business

This is what happens when a client arrives at Pura Group with a project. We don't sell loose steps; we build the complete path. And if you only need one of these blocks, we work that way too.

01

Business Validation & Strategy

Before investing a single dollar in advertising or technology, we analyze whether your idea has a real market. We study the product, sector, competition and ideal client. We define the positioning, business model and action plan.

  • Market research and competitive analysis
  • Ideal client definition and pain/benefit map
  • Value proposition and core message
  • Business strategy and roadmap
02

Brand, Identity & Copywriting

We create the visual and verbal identity of your company. Logo, palette, typography, tone of voice and key messages. A brand that conveys credibility, coherence and differentiation from the first contact.

  • Full branding: logo, colors, typography, brand guide
  • Strategic copywriting: texts that sell and position
  • Communication adapted to each channel and audience
03

Web Development & Digital Presence

We design and develop your corporate website, landing pages, online stores or applications with one goal: to work. Fast, secure, optimized for search engines and ready to convert.

  • Corporate websites, landings and e-commerce
  • Conversion-oriented design (CRO)
  • Technical SEO and organic positioning
  • CRM and automation tool integrations
04

Client Acquisition & Digital Advertising

We design and manage campaigns on Meta (Facebook and Instagram) and Google Ads. We create the ads, texts, creatives and audiences. We measure every dollar invested and continuously optimize.

  • Meta Ads strategy and management
  • Google Ads: search, display and remarketing
  • Ad creation and advertising creatives
  • Analysis, reporting and continuous optimization
05

Process Automation & CRM

We eliminate repetitive tasks from your business. We implement systems that automatically manage leads, send personalized communications and keep your CRM updated without manual intervention.

  • CRM implementation and configuration
  • Workflow automation and lead tracking
  • Email marketing and automated sequences
  • Integrations between tools and platforms
06

Digital Strategy & Content Marketing

We design your company's comprehensive digital strategy: which channels to use, what content to create, how to position yourself and how to measure that everything works. We manage social media and build community around your brand.

  • 360° digital marketing strategy
  • Social media and content management
  • Web analytics and dashboards
  • Content marketing and brand positioning
07

Analytics, Reporting & Business Intelligence

Data without analysis is useless. We build custom dashboards, executive reports and KPI tracking systems so you make decisions based on real information, not intuition.

  • Custom business dashboards
  • Periodic executive reporting
  • KPI and key metrics tracking
  • Campaign and channel performance analysis
08

Global Consulting & Collaboration Models

We adapt to you. We work as an external team, as an extension of your internal team or as a strategic partner. If you have a project that needs direction and execution, let's talk.

  • Strategic consulting for expanding companies
  • Complex digital project management
  • Flexible models: fees, retainer or revenue share
