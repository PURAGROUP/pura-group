COOKIE POLICY

This Cookie Policy forms part of the Legal Notice and Privacy Policy of the PURA GROUP LLC website.

The purpose of this policy is to clearly and simply inform you about the use of cookies on this website.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website installs on your device when you browse it. They are used to remember information about your visit, facilitate navigation, improve user experience, and ensure the proper functioning of the site.

Some cookies are necessary for the basic operation of the website, while others are used for statistical or service improvement purposes.

2. Types of cookies we use

On the PURA GROUP LLC website, we use our own and third-party cookies for the following purposes:

  • Technical or necessary cookies: These allow the basic functioning of the website and do not require user consent.
  • Analytics or performance cookies: These help analyze website usage and improve its performance.
  • Personalization cookies: These allow basic user preferences to be remembered, such as language or display settings.
  • Marketing or measurement cookies (optional): These are used to analyze campaigns and commercial actions when advertising tools are employed.

In no case do the cookies used store sensitive information or allow the direct personal identification of the user.

3. Third-party cookies

This website may use third-party tools that install their own cookies, such as:

  • Website traffic analysis tools (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Advertising or campaign tracking tools.
  • Technical platforms necessary for site operation (such as payment gateways or automation systems, if applicable).

These third parties may process information in an aggregated and anonymous manner in accordance with their own privacy policies.

PURA GROUP LLC selects providers that comply with international data protection standards but is not responsible for the content or specific policies of such third parties.

4. Managing and disabling cookies

Users may accept or reject non-essential cookies through the cookie banner displayed when accessing the website.

Additionally, cookies can be deleted or blocked through browser settings:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data.
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data.
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data.
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions.

Disabling certain cookies may affect the proper functioning of some parts of the website.

5. Consent

Non-essential cookies are only installed once the user has provided explicit consent through the cookie banner.

Continued use of the website without accepting non-essential cookies will not result in their installation.

6. Cookie management system

The PURA GROUP LLC website uses a cookie management system that allows users to accept or reject the use of non-essential cookies in a simple manner through the banner displayed upon accessing the website.

7. Policy updates

PURA GROUP LLC reserves the right to modify this Cookie Policy at any time to adapt it to legal, technical, or functional changes.

Updates will be published on this page and will take effect from the date of publication.

En el sitio web de PURA GROUP LLC, utilizamos cookies propias y de terceros para los siguientes fines:
